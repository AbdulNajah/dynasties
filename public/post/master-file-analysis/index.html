Political Families

This data set knits the families of contestants in the elections held in Uttar Pradesh. This includes Loksabha, Vidhnsabha, Panchayat Raj institutions and Urban local body elections.

Analysis goals

  • Exploration within the political families dataset
  • Exploring how political families are associated with relevant variables in the other datas ets

All elections

This universe includes winners and runner-ups from the general and assembly elections from the year 1977 to 2019 and the winners from the local body elections held from 1995 to 2015 in the state of Uttar Pradesh. This contains 18340 observations and the number unique individuals is 10915 and the families is 1083. It has to be noted that 88% of this winners are just patriarchs.

In this universe of families 2308 winners are part of some family and 783 winners out of this is just patriarchs while the rest is other family members.

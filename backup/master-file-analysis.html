AE - GE

This universe includes winners and runner-ups in the general and assembly elections from the year 1977 to 2019. This contains 11550 observations and the number unique individuals is 5286 and the families is 209. It has to be noted that 93% of this winners are just patriarchs.

In this universe of families 1631 winners and runner-ups are part of some family and 903 winners and runner-ups out of this is just patriarchs while the rest is other family members.
